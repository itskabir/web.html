BTIRT COLLAGE

Babulal Tarabai (BT) group of Educational Institutions, under registered society LSES (Little Star Education Society, founded in 1993).
This group consists of “BTIRT (Babulal Tarabai Institute of Research and Technology 2008), BTIE (Babulal Tarabai Institute of Excellence2006), BTIPS (Babulal Tarabai Institute of Pharmaceutical Science 2019), BTM (Babulal Tarabai Mahavidyalaya 2019) and LSSM (Little Star Shailesh Memorial Senior Secondary School Sagar 1992)”,
progressing under the chairmanship of visionary Mr. Santosh Jain ‘Ghari’. Little Star Education Society was founded with a pious objective of providing quality education for the benefit of society and the nation as a whole.
In the year of formation of education society, it established “Little Star Shailesh Memorial Senior Secondary School providing education to children from Nursery to 12th standard.
